Mission Control App

Tablet or smart phone app that allows you to plan and execute autonomous data collection with our field scout, or with your personal DJI UAS. This system allows you to find and delineate your field using overhead satellite imaging. With easy to use touch screen controls, you can set way points, or allow the system to automatically generate flight paths based on the size and shape of your field. Available soon!

High Resolution Field Scout

Innovative field scout system that utilizes high and low altitude data collection modes, with one of a kind on-site data pre-processing that provides you with near real-time field situation report. This system will allow you keep up with the dynamic changes in your crops, and make time-critical decisions faster than ever before. Available soon!

High Efficiency Variable Rate Sprayer

Simple to use, autonomous variable rate chemical application drone. With the ability to differentiate between crops and weeds, this system offers chemical application with pinpoint accuracy, providing the potential for unprecedented efficiency in farming. Available soon!
