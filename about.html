About Us

Situated at the heart of the American Cornbelt, Ag-Sensus was founded by pioneers in the precision agriculture field. With the goal of making precision agriculture technology accessible to hard-working American farmers, Ag-Sensus will provide equipment and services geared towards improving yield efficiency by providing users with practical tools with which to make smarter decisions.


With exclusive license to research conducted by the Illinois Laboratory for Agriculture Remote Sensing over the past decade, and established through the University of Illinois Research Park's I-Start program, Ag-Sensus is well positioned to leverage the recent technological advances in UAS technology, and agriculture remote sensing expertise at the University of Illinois into practical precision agriculture solutions.
